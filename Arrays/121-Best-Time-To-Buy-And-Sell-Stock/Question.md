# 121. Best Time to Buy and Sell Stock

Difficulty: Easy

You are given an array prices where prices[i] is the price of a stock on the ith day.

Choose a single day to buy one stock and choose a different day in the future to sell that stock.

Return the maximum profit you can achieve.

Example:

Input:
prices = [7,1,5,3,6,4]

Output:
5

Explanation:
Buy at price 1 and sell at price 6.
Profit = 6 - 1 = 5
